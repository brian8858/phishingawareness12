## Security Awareness Program (90-day) — LMS Outline

### Module 1: Phishing Fundamentals (Week 1)
- Goal: Recognize core phishing indicators and reporting process
- Lessons:
  - What is phishing? Channels (email, SMS, voice, QR)
  - Red flags: sender, domains, urgency, attachments, links
  - How to report: using the Report Phish button and mailbox
- Assessment: 10-question quiz; hands-on link-hover exercise

### Module 2: Email Anatomy & Safe Verification (Week 2)
- Goal: Inspect headers and URLs; verify requests safely
- Lessons:
  - URL patterns, lookalike domains, shorteners
  - Header basics and reply-to tricks
  - Safe nav: bookmarks, portals, out-of-band checks
- Lab: Inspect 5 sample emails; classify risk

### Module 3: OAuth Consent & Account Security (Week 4)
- Goal: Spot malicious consent prompts; protect accounts
- Lessons:
  - OAuth consent screens and risky permissions
  - Revoking third-party access
  - MFA best practices (no QR from emails)
- Assessment: Identify risky permissions scenario

### Module 4: E‑Signature & Document Lures (Week 5)
- Goal: Validate document requests
- Lessons:
  - Common e-sign lures and timing pressure
  - Verifying via official portal/bookmarks
- Lab: Compare sample emails to find mismatches

### Module 5: Delivery & Payment Scams (Week 7)
- Goal: Handle delivery notices and fee requests
- Lessons:
  - Tracking formats, fake fees, mismatched domains
  - Safe checks via typed-in official sites
- Assessment: Choose-the-safe-action scenarios

### Module 6: SSO/IdP Changes & IT Notices (Week 9)
- Goal: Validate identity prompts and IT announcements
- Lessons:
  - Normal SSO URLs and login paths
  - Change verification via IT hub
- Lab: Identify fake vs. real SSO notices

### Microlearnings (sprinkled after campaigns)
- 3–5 min videos: QR “quishing,” report-quality tips, hover-before-click
- Just-in-time page linked from campaign landing redirects

### Capstone & Metrics (Week 12)
- Capstone quiz (20 Q) + simulated phish reflection
- KPI targets: click <8%, cred submit <2%, report >25%, time-to-report <15m
- Resources: one-pagers, reporting guide, policy links
